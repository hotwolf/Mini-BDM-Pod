--- layout: post title: D-Bug12XZv6 released ---

Version 6 of the D-Bug12 Firmware is now available for download at the Frescale web site .
D-Bug12XZv6 is the new recommended firmware for the Mini-BDM-Pod.
In addition to the features of the previous version (D-Bug12Xv5 ), Verson 6 supports NXP’s new MagniV devices (incl. the new S12Z CPU).
